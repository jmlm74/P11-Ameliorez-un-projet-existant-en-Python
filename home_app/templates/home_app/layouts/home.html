Pur Beurre is the initiative of two lovers of French gastronomy, Colette Tatou and Rémy, that you have certainly encountered in our excellent restaurant Ratatouille.

Colette Tatou: "We all love to eat, especially in France! Personally, a baguette fresh, good goat cheese and a good red wine are enough for me. Like everyone, I do my shopping at the supermarket. But little by little I realized, especially when we opened the restaurant, that I could replace the sanitized products that I had used to buying with good local products And often for the same price! "

Remy: "For my part, I was already going to many stores because I like to go for a walk, especially in cellars and kitchens. I knew the contents of organic stores by heart (which I love) as well as the ingredients of all the foods sold. When Colette told me asked if she could replace her morning Nutella with a healthier food, I knew it all now where to find it. The idea of ​​Pur Beurre was born! "
